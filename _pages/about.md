About me
======
Hi, I am Kubilay! I am a fourth year PhD student in Electrical Engineering at New York University [Tandon School of Engineering](https://engineering.nyu.edu). I am part of the [NYU Wireless](https://wireless.engineering.nyu.edu) research center and [CommIT](https://wp.nyu.edu/elza_erkip/) group under the advisory of Elza Erkip. I have received my B.S. in Electrical &#38; Electronics Engineering and Physics (Double Major) from [Boğaziçi University](https://bogazici.edu.tr/en_US) (Istanbul, Turkey).
